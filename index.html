About me

I am post-doctoral researcher at the Division of Decision and Control systems, School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, KTH, Royal Instiute of Technology, Stockholm, working with Prof. Karl Henrik Johansson. I received my Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from the University of Maryland, College park (UMD) in 2020 under the supervision of Prof. John Baras. Prior to my current position, I was a post-doctoral researcher at the Institute for Systems Research,UMD.
