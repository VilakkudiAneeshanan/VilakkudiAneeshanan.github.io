Bio

I am a post-doc at the DCS division, KTH, Stockholm, working with Prof. Karl Henrik Johansson. I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Maryland, College park (UMD), U.S.A in 2020 under the supervision of Prof. John Baras. I received my Bachelor degree in Instrumentation Engineeering from Indian Insitute of Technology, Kharagpur, India in 2013. Prior to my current position, I was a post-doctoral researcher at the Institute for Systems Research,UMD.
